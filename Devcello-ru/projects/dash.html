Dash!

Description:

A casual mobile game inspired by "Duet" and a level from "Just shapes and beats".

You play as a cyan cube, the objective is to escape the lava slowly going up and dodge all the white blocks that fall on you.

The first ever game I tried to finish but couldn't due to the lack of experience.

It's very unlikely that I will ever finish it since due to my low experience back then, the code turned out to be very messy. So even if I decide to finish, I'll have to start everything over.
